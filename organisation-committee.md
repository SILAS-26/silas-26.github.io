# Organisation Committee
* Pepijn de Reus is a guest researcher at the University of Amsterdam's Complex Cyber Infrastructure
 research group. Pepijn co-organised last year's workshop on SusPETs
* Maja H. Kirkeby is an Associate Professor at Roskilde University, Denmark. Recently, she has served as co-organiser of the 2024 Dagstuhl Seminar “Power, Energy, and Carbon-Aware Computing on Heterogeneous Systems”, GreenAI'25, the Workshop on Resource Awareness of Systems and Society 2024, and the QUATIC '24 track "Sustainability as a Quality Concern (SQC)".
* Fernando Castor is an Associate Professor at the University of Twente, The Netherlands. Fernando has served as workshop co-chair for ICT4S'22
 and co-organiser of the ASE 2021 AeSIR workshop, among many other events.
* Ana Oprescu is an Assistant Professor at Universiteit van Amsterdam. She co-organised SATToSE'20 Amsterdam (the first online edition due to COVID-19) and co-organised the ICT4S 2024 SusPETs workshop.
* João Paulo Fernandes is a Visiting Associate Professor at New York University Abu Dhabi. In areas related to this proposal, João served as General Co-chair for ICT4S'22 and as co-organiser for GreenAI'25 and last year's SusPETs. Beyond these topics, he also served as General Co-chair for IFL'23 and VL/HCC'18.
